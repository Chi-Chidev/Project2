About Esther

My name is Esther Chinonye. I'm a product designer with aspirations to become a full-stack developer. My journey into the tech world began in 2022, and it's been a rollercoaster ride challenging yet incredibly rewarding.

I consider myself to be a blend of introvert and extrovert, depending on my environment and the people around me. In the right setting, I can be very outgoing and sociable, while other times, I enjoy quiet moments ofreflection and solitude.

There's so much more to me than what you'll read here, so I encourage you to get to know me personally. In the meantime, here's a sneak peek into my world.
